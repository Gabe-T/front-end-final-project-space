Welcome to

Take some time to learn about the cosmos!

Navigate through a 5+ year archive of NASA photos and accompanying descriptions using the buttons above.

The panel below allows you to create an ambient soundscape to accompany your journey through space.

Press "start" to activate the sound generator.

Hover over each parameter for a brief description of its function. Load a patch to hear what others have come up with, or just dive in!


This site developed by Joseph Sardashti, Clint Langwell, and Gabe Tarrant as a Grand Circus Bootcamp Final Project, Dec. 2020

Synth component made using Tone.js and Tonal.js libraries. Huge thank you to Dan Tramte and David Bouchard for their helpful tutorials.
